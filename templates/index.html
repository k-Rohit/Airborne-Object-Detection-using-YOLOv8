Airborne Object Detection

Welcome to our website dedicated to airborne object detection using YOLOv8. Our platform offers an accurate and efficient means of detecting airplanes, helicopters, and drones in real-time. We have developed a state-of-the-art deep learning model that is capable of identifying these objects with exceptional precision and speed. Our platform has the potential to contribute significantly to the enhancement of aviation safety, security, and environmental monitoring.
